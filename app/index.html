Ophthalmology is the study of medical conditions relating to the eye. Ophthalmologists specialize in treating eye health problems, such as dry eye syndrome, eyelid conditions such as blepharitis and styes, cataracts, diabetic eye disease, glaucoma or macular degeneration. Timely and high-quality treatment of eye diseases can save not only health, but also make life more fulfilling.

An ophthalmologist can also guide healthy lifestyle choices. You can protect your vision by eating eye-healthy foods, exercising, and protecting your eyes from UV light. Cigarette smoke in particular can worsen eye conditions such as cataracts and macular degeneration. Our doctor will suggest personalized tips.

When to go to the ophthalmologist

With what complaints and pathologies you need to see a doctor

If you have at least one of the following symptoms, you should immediately contact a specialist so that the treatment is timely and most effective:

  • You are having vision changes
  • You are having eye dryness
  • Your eyes are red/bloodshot
  • You have pain in your eyes
  • You have headaches/pressure in your eyes
  • Considering lenses or glasses
  • Considering LASIK or other refractive surgery
  • You have diabetes

Also, our ophthalmologist will help you in the treatment of the following diseases:

  • Refractive Errors
  • Age-Related Macular Degeneration
  • Cataract
  • Diabetic Retinopathy
  • Glaucoma
  • Amblyopia
  • Strabismus
  • Any allergic reactions associated with the eyes
